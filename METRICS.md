# Products Matrics

- **North Star Metric:** % of Audits with >$200 Indentified Savings.
- **Why:** This metric directly correlates with Credex's goal of lead generation for credit sales.
- **Input Metrics:**
1. Audit Completion Rate (From Start vs, Result Shown).
2. Lead Capture Conversion (Email Submitted after result).
3. Share Rate (Link copies per unique visitor).
