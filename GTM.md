# Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy

## Target User
- **Persona:** Engineering Managers and Operation Leads at Seed-to-Series A Startups.
- **Trigger:** They just received their monthly credit card bill and noticed a $500+ surge in AI spend (Cursor/Claude/ChatGPT).

## Distribution Channels
1. **Niche Communities:** Direct outreach in 'Indie Hackers' and 'Buildspace' Discord Servers where developers complain about Claude usage limits.
2. **LinkedIn Micro-Influencers:** Engaging with experts like Aditya Swaroop and Meet Aghera who build open-source AI Clients. 
3. **"Calculators as Content":** Promoting the tool as a "CFO's best friend" on Twitter (X) threads explaining AI token economics.

## Success Metrics
- **North Star Metric:** % of users who copy the 'Shareable URL' after lead capture.
- **Week 1 Target:** 100 successful audits with a 20% lead capture rate.
