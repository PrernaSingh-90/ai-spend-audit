# Unit Economics & Growth Roadmap

## Lead Valuation
- **Estimated Savings Per Audit:** $1,200/year (Average for a small startup).
- **Credex Commission:** If Credex facilitates credit purchases at a 10% margin, a converted lead is worth **$120/year** in direct revenue.
- **Strategic Value:** High-savings leads are prime targets for Credex's high-ticket consulting services, potentially worth **$1,000+ per conversion.**

## Conversion Funnel
1. - **Audit Completed:** 100%
2. - **Lead Captured (Email):** 25% (Benchmark for high-value free tools).
3. - **Credex Consultation:** 5% (For audits showing >$500 savings).
4. - **Credit Purchase:** 2% conversion from initial audit.


## Path to $1M ARR (18 Months)
To reach $1M Annual Recurring Revenue, We need: 
- **Annual target:** 8,333 credit-buying customers (at $120 avg commission).
- **Strategy:** Partner with VC firms and Accelerator programs (Y Combinator, Techstars) to offer the tool as a "CFO Audit" during their batch orientation. This creates a high-volume, zero-CAC distribution channel.


## CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **Organic (Reddit/X/LinkedIn):** $0.
- **Paid (LinkedIn Ads targeting Founders):** Estimated $40 - $60 per captured lead.
- **LTV/CAC Ratio:** Targeted at 3:1 for sustainable growth.
